Stargazing

Lectures last around 30 minutes plus a 15-minute Q&A. If the weather permits (see chart below), they are followed by star-gazing through our many telescopes. We also offer hot chocolate (while supplies last) and informal Q&A with astronomers outside of the lecture setting. Attendance varies from 50-300 people depending on the weather and the topic. Lectures, slideshows, and star-gazing are aimed to be accessible to all ages.
